Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your Taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped whit sweet Caramelized Onions, tart
Cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

featured in:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 taspoon instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cups lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

for the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1-2 tablespoon baisamic vinegar
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubers
  • 1/2 cups fresh or frozen
